Deer



Deer are browsers, and feed primarily on leaves, Most deer bear 32 teeth

  • Scientific Name: Cervidae
  • Average Length: 85-150 cm
  • Average Lifspan: about 12 years
  • Habitat: grasslands, prairies and plains, mountains, and forest

a hoofed grazing or browsing animal, with branched bony antlers that are shed annually and typically borne only by the male.
